Constitution of Capitalist Paradise


To all present, and to all eyes who see, Greetings!

Preamble

Bearing now in these pages a CONSTITUTION of governance and law cast upon by inheritance and virtue all NationStates comprising the region CAPITALIST PARADISE

Our Motto:

From Liberty, Prosperity

From Prosperity, Freedom

Rights and Bylaws

Rights of Sovereignty

  • No nation shall be judged or reprimanded or expelled for its choice of religion or absence thereof -Regional authority shall remain secular in nature
  • No nation shall be judged or reprimanded or expelled for its NationStates political category
  • No nation shall be judged or reprimanded or expelled for its Racial predominance
    • and others to be brought by amendment through the legislative process

Inherent Rights of Membership

  • No tax, tariff or monetary penalty shall be imposed by regional authority
  • To vie and compete for political office
  • To be heard by the Arbiter in intra-regional disputes
  • To be represented by the Arbiter in civil and criminal cause, and investigation and trial; any nation that has been ejected from Capitalist Paradise may request a trial by the Supreme Court through the Arbiter
  • To post respectfully on the forum.
  • To post on the message board, respectfully and in accordance with game rules of etiquette
  • To bring for consideration in the Legislature any issue (By-law) of timely relevance
  • To have a vote in the Legislature[1] Every individual member/player with 15 days continuous presence in Capitalist Paradise shall have a right to one vote in all Legislative proceedings
    • and others to be brought by amendment through the legislative process

By-Laws

Regional authority shall issue no dictate concerning a Nations Motto, National Leader, Flag, Capital City, Religion, National Animal or Currency

Government

The governing body of Capitalist Paradise shall consist of four general offices:
  • The Executive
  • The Legislative
  • The Judicial
  • The Vocals
    • -And additional offices created as needed

The Executive

The Founder and President

Duties include but are not limited to:
  • Executor of regional membership
  • Enforcement and defense of this Constitution and the common defense of Capitalist Paradise
  • In the event of raid invasion, the founder may may assume Full Regional Control for the eradication of offending parties
  • Will maintain a Chronicle of significant events in Capitalist Paradises' History

World Assembly Delegate And CEO

Qualification: This position requires a minimum influence level of "Vassal" and is elected by plurality votes of confidence as indicated by World Assembly members, for a term of service of not less than 90 days. After 90 days any qualified WA Nation desirous to challenge may and must lay challenge by precluding challenge publicly on the Capitalist Paradise Regional Message Board.. Upon challenge, this electoral process is adopted:

  1. Introduction and Debate: A window of 10 days is opened, beginning on a Friday for candidates to debate publicly on the Capitalist Paradise Regional Message Board and for WA member nations to endorse/unendorse the candidate(s) of their choosing.
  2. Flux: During these 10 days the WA Delegate may naturally switch between candidates according to endorsements received or lost. The Founder, at his discretion, may reserve administrative powers at this time.
  3. Appointment: On the 11th day morning, a Monday morning, the successful candidate will be indicated and commence his/her 90 day term of office as World Assembly Delegate of the Region Capitalist Paradise.

An accidental usurper, passing the Delegate to this position must immediately act to reduce its endorsement count or withdraw from the World Assembly, or suffer ejection by the Founder and criminally charged as a common "Raider". In the event the WA Delegate loses his seat as a result of endorsement withdraws, regional authority shall take no punitive action against the accidental usurper. However, the Founder may, at his discretion restrict access to regional controls until a new Delegate is lawfully elected. For the purpose of this document, "Raider" is defined as any nation which acquires WA Delegate status without following the above qualifications and electoral guidelines.

Duties of the WA Delegate include but are not limited to:

  • Enforcement and defense of this Constitution and the common defense of Capitalist Paradise
  • Representing our region to the World Assembly
  • Executor of regional membership
  • Maintenance of the World Fact Book Entry
  • In the event of raid invasion, the WA Delegate may assume Full Regional Control for the eradication of offending parties
  • In the event of raid/invasion and with the absence of the Founder, the Delegate may, at his/her discretion appoint a Minister of Defense whos duties are limited to:
    • Marshaling a defense force from within and without the Region for the purpose of re-seating the rightful Delegate
    • Organizing and effecting retaliation upon the offenders homelands, at the discretion of the Delegate

Minister of Foreign Affairs

This position is appointed by and serves at the pleasure of the WA Delegate. This Cabinet member must be a member of the WA. Duties include but are not limited to:

  • Serves in the capacity of the Delegate in the absence of the Delegate
  • Enforcement and defense of this Constitution and the common defense of Capitalist Paradise
  • General management of extra-regional affairs
  • Muster, train and maintain an extra-regional investigation team
  • Appointment of Ambassadorships whos duties include but are not limited to:
    • To maintain an active inter-regional dialogue between Capitalist Paradise and all Capitalist Paradise Embassy host regions
    • To recommend the closure of their respective embassy should the diplomatic relationship prove detrimental to the values or integrity Capitalist Paradise

Minister of the Interior

This position is appointed by and serves at the pleasure of the WA Delegate. Duties include but are not limited to:

  • Responsible for recruitment and retention efforts
  • General management of intra-regional affairs
  • Maintenance of the off-site forum
  • Monitor the Forums and Regional Message Board for abuse, with respect to the rules of our game

Chief Editor[2]

This position is appointed by and serves at the pleasure of the WA Delegate. Duties include but are not limited to:

  • Responsible for content published in the Regional Newspaper
  • General management of regional roleplays
  • Mentor new players on how to roleplay properly
  • Appointment of additional Editors whose duties include but are not limited to:
    • Assist the Chief Editor in his/her duties

The Chief Editor and/or Editors may be removed from office by a majority vote by the Legislature or at the discretion of the WA Delegate.

The Legislative Body

President of the Legislature

The POL is elected to serve a period of 90 days. After this term the office is open to challenge by any Capitalist Paradise member nation; by popular vote; by the legislature, vote to be counted by a two member panel appointed by the WA Delegate; Duties include but are not limited to:

  • Presentation of issues and elections to the Legislature
  • Shall appoint two vote counters for tally of votes
  • OR At his/her discretion, utilize an off site polling service
  • Presentation of vote results to the Delegate for ratification into law
  • Enforcement and defense of this Constitution and the common defense of Capitalist Paradise

The Legislature

The full body of Capitalist Paradise member nations with at least 15 days continuous presence within Capitalist Paradise.[1]

The full body of Capitalist Paradise member players with at least 15 days continuous presence within Capitalist Paradise.

Duties are:

  • Presenting issues of timely relevance including by-laws and structural changes to this Constitution, to the POL for presentation to the entire voting body. The Author of any legislation is responsible for its promotion.
  • Voting issues and elections as presented -participation is not mandatory
  • Enforcement and defense of this Constitution and the common defense of Capitalist Paradise

The Judiciary

The Supreme Court

A three Juror panel, including and led by a Chief Justice
Advancement to Chief Justice is by seniority
Appointment is for life or retirement, however subject to the authority of The Vocals.
Duties are:

  • Enforcement and defense of this Constitution
  • Resolve civil disputes among member nations if and after all reasonable efforts of the Advocate have been exhausted
  • Resolve criminal complaints.
  • Refer guilty parties to the CEO for ejection, banning or banning and ejection from the region if warranted; in the opinion of the panel

A fourth position "Alternate Justice" shall be elected by the Legislature and seated by the WA Delegate.

In the event of a SC Jurors retirement, the Alternate Justice shall advance to junior-most member of the panel. In the event of temporary absence or conflict of interest of a full juror, the Alternate shall hear the case and he/she shall have a vote among the panel

Prosecutor

Appointed by the Chief Justice of the Supreme Court on a case by case basis. Duties are as the title describes.

Advocate and Arbiter General

Elected by the Legislature to serve a term of not less than 90 days. After this term the office is open to challenge by any Capitalist Paradise member nation; by popular vote of the legislature: confirmed and seated by the WA Delegate.
Duties are:

  • Vigorously represent defendants in all criminal cases
  • Resolve civil disputes among member nations
  • Provide final defense appeal to the WA Delegate in capital cases

The Vocals

A panel, consisting of former Capitalist Paradise WA Delegates, with a minimum of 90 days in service in the WA Delegate position, residing in Capitalist Paradise whos duties are limited to:

  • Convene on a bi-monthly basis
  • Evaluate the performance of the Founder and provide constructive feedback to the Founder
  • Evaluate the performance of the WA Delegate and provide constructive feedback to the WA Delegate.
  • upon request by any Capitalist Paradise member nation, this panel shall review the performance of any member of the Supreme Court. If, in the opinion of the panel that impeachment is warranted, the panel shall, through the legislative process, conduct a vote of no-confidence. Should "no-confidence" be established, this panel shall petition the WA Delegate for evacuation of the offending juror. The WA Delegate is not bound by this petition.

By-laws

Emergency Powers Act

Sunday, November 24th, 2013

Purpose: To provide a way to streamline the courts in the event of a emergency situation

Legislation Text:

In the event of the absence of two or more members of the Supreme Court, the WA Delegate may appoint temporary justices to fill the place of the missing members. Appointees must be citizens of the Capitalist Paradise and members of the World Assembly, and can serve as a temporary replacement for no longer than 30 days.

Absence of a member of the Supreme Court is defined as an inability to contact or receive communications from a member of the court for 7 days after a notice for their presence is made. A notice must be made via telegram.

Note: If the absent member is the Chief Justice, the senior most member of the court is also the temporary Chief Justice until the Chief Justice returns. If there are no members of the court whatsoever, the WA Delegate may cast a vote as a tie-breaker (but only if needed), and there is no Chief Justice.

If the member of the Supreme Court returns while the temporary replacement justice is still active, the temporary justice has 48 hours to send all (public and private) court communiques received in the sitting member's absence, and transfer power back to the sitting member in that same time-frame.
